Vigilance 360

Vigilance 360 is a comprehensive solution to address security threats in Automated Teller Machines (ATM), focusing on pre-incident detection, identifying individuals attempting entry with covered faces. It uses dual-camera surveillance to detect and store entry and exit time. The system detects and alerts authorities about individuals carrying harmful tools. Occupancy monitoring, another use-case of the system, helps maintain a safe environment, and a user-specific session duration enhances operational efficiency. ATM Environment integrates pre-incident detection, real-time detection, and occupancy management to provide a robust solution against diverse security threats. This system ensures a secure and protected ATM environment.
